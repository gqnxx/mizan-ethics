# MIZAN Protocol · بروتوكول ميزان 🔇

**A quantitative framework for non-invasive OSINT compliance.**

*Measure the ethical risk before you collect. Stay invisible while you work. Prove you did it right when it's over.*

---

## ⚖️ The Solution: MIZAN

MIZAN (ميزان) is Arabic for "balance" or "scale." It turns OSINT ethics from a vague "gut feeling" into a measurable engineering standard.

### 1. Blast Radius Calculus (BRC)
A mathematical formula to score ethical risk before execution:
**ERS = Φ(S, A, E)**
- **S (Sensitivity):** How private is the data?
- **A (Adjacency):** How far is this person from the target? (Weighted heavily for regional social structures).
- **E (Entropy):** How much noise does the collection method make?

### 2. Minimal-Interaction Evidence Synthesis (MIES)
A methodology for zero-touch collection. Cache-first strategies, randomized fingerprinting, and temporal spacing to ensure you remain a ghost.

### 3. Proof-of-Ethical-Conduct (PoEC)
A digital chain-of-custody that tags every piece of evidence with its source, access method, ERS score, and an integrity hash.
